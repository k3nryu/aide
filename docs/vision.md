# Vision

Aide is a personal executive assistant system designed to help one person run their day with less friction.

It should become a trusted place to answer:

- What matters today?
- What should I avoid doing?
- What have I been thinking about?
- What needs follow-up?
- What is happening with my money?
- What should my assistant remember for later?

## Product Principles

### Capture First

It should be easy to quickly capture a task, not-to-do, thought, expense, or reminder without deciding where it belongs immediately.

### Briefings Over Dashboards

Aide should summarize the day rather than merely display data. The daily briefing is the center of the product: tasks, constraints, recent thoughts, money notes, and eventually AI-generated context.

### ADHD-Friendly Workflow

The system should reduce planning overhead. It should favor short lists, visible priorities, gentle constraints, and not-to-dos alongside todos.

### Personal Memory

Over time, Aide should remember useful facts, recurring themes, decisions, and preferences. Memory should be inspectable and correctable.

### Practical AI

AI should support real workflows: summarize, retrieve, remind, organize, and connect ideas. It should not obscure the user's data or make important decisions without review.

## Core Domains

### Daily Briefing

A daily view that surfaces today's tasks, relevant not-to-dos, recent thoughts, and useful summaries.

### Tasks

Task management should distinguish To-Do from Not-to-Do. To-Dos can be one-time or recurring, while Not-to-Dos represent ongoing boundaries and should not have a completion state. Aide should keep capture simple, then evaluate priority from importance, urgency, due dates, recurrence, and context.

### Not-To-Dos

Intentional constraints and avoidance items. These are first-class records rather than hidden notes.

### Thoughts

Fast capture for ideas, notes, reflections, and fragments that may later become tasks or memory.

### Finance

Lightweight income and expense tracking. The first goal is useful record keeping, not full accounting.

### AI Memory

Future retrieval-augmented memory that helps Aide connect today's work to past thoughts, tasks, and decisions.
